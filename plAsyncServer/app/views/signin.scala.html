Why am I Being Asked to Sign In?

This plAsync server does not recognize your device. It is asking you to sign in so it can link your identity with your device. As long as you sign in the same way from each device, you will be able to participate in multiplayer games hosted by this server from any device.

Why Do You Need my Email?

If you are using Google, we need your email address because Google OpenIDs are specific to the domain that requested them. This could cause problems with identifying you across devices, so we use your email to verify your identity. plAsync wil not display or share your email with anyone.

Why OpenID?

It’s a single username and password that allows you to log in to any OpenID-enabled site.

It works on thousands of websites.

It’s an open standard.
